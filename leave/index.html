How to Apply for Leave Online on HRMS Bihar — Step by Step

The HRMS Bihar leave application form online is inside the main portal — no separate URL needed. HRMS leave management login uses your regular Employee ID and password. Here is the complete process.

HRMS Bihar Leave Management — Dashboard View

  1. Open Google Chrome and go to hrms.bihar.gov.in. Login with your Employee ID and password. If you need help with login, see our complete HRMS Bihar login guide.
  2. On your dashboard, look for Leave Management in the navigation menu. Click it. This is your HRMS leave management section — no separate login needed.
  3. Check your Leave Balance first — click Leave Balance before applying. This shows your available days for each leave type. Applying without checking is the most common mistake people make — it leads to auto-rejection.
  4. Click Apply for Leave. The HRMS Bihar leave application form opens.
  5. From the Leave Type dropdown, select the type you need — Earned Leave, Casual Leave, Medical Leave, LWP, Maternity Leave, Child Care Leave, etc.
  6. Enter From Date and To Date. The system automatically calculates number of days and shows how much balance will remain after approval.
  7. Enter your Reason clearly. For Medical Leave attach doctor's certificate. For Child Care Leave, attach the CCL affidavit. For Study Leave, attach the institution letter.
  8. Click Submit. Your DDO receives the application automatically with a notification. You get a confirmation SMS on your registered mobile number. Note the Application Reference Number shown on screen.
✅ HRMS Online Leave Application — Mobile Works Too
The HRMS Bihar leave management section works on mobile browser. Open hrms.bihar.gov.in in Google Chrome on your Android or iPhone — Apply for Leave, check status, cancel leave — everything works. No app download needed for web access. However, the official HRMS Bihar mobile app also supports leave applications — available on Google Play Store.

Check Leave Balance & Application Status

HRMS Bihar Leave Balance — Available Days for Each Leave Type

How to Check Leave Balance

  1. Login to hrms.bihar.gov.in and go to Leave Management.
  2. Click Leave Balance. All your available leave days appear — Earned Leave, Casual Leave, Half Pay Leave, and others — in a single table.
  3. Note down your available balance for the type you want to apply. Apply only if sufficient balance is available to avoid rejection.

How to Check Leave Application Status

  1. Login → Leave Management → My Leave Applications or Leave Status.
  2. All your submitted applications appear with the current status — Pending Approval, Approved, or Rejected.
  3. For Rejected applications, your DDO's rejection reason is visible. Read it, address the reason, and reapply.

LWP in HRMS Bihar — Leave Without Pay Explained

Many employees search "lwp in hrms" without fully understanding what it means for their service record and salary. Here is what you need to know before applying for LWP.

LWP stands for Leave Without Pay. On the HRMS Bihar portal it may appear as LWP, EOL (Extraordinary Leave), or simply Leave Without Pay in the leave type dropdown. It is granted in two situations — either when all your paid leave balance is exhausted, or when you voluntarily want unpaid leave even if paid leave is available.

⚠️ LWP — Critical Things to Know Before Applying
1. Full salary deducted for every day of LWP — including DA and all allowances.
2. Qualifying service — LWP period generally does not count towards qualifying service for pension calculation.
3. Increment impact — If total LWP in a year exceeds limits, it may defer your annual increment.
4. DDO approval required — LWP is not auto-granted. Your DDO must approve it.

How to Apply for LWP on HRMS Bihar

  1. Login to hrms.bihar.gov.in → Leave Management. First check your Leave Balance to confirm all paid leave types are exhausted or you specifically want LWP.
  2. Click Apply for Leave. In the Leave Type dropdown select LWP or Extraordinary Leave or Leave Without Pay.
  3. Enter your leave dates and write a clear, specific reason. LWP applications require stronger justification than paid leaves — mention the exact circumstances.
  4. Submit the application. Your DDO reviews it. Unlike paid leave, DDOs scrutinise LWP applications more carefully.
  5. If approved, the LWP period will show a salary deduction on your next month's pay slip. Check our salary certificate guide to verify deductions.

Maternity Leave Rules in Bihar Government

Many women government employees in Bihar are not fully aware of their maternity leave entitlement. The rules are actually quite employee-friendly — and they apply on the HRMS Bihar portal the same way as any other leave.

CategoryMaternity Leave EntitlementPay During Leave
First / Second Child26 weeks (182 days)Full pay
Third Child onwards12 weeks (84 days)Full pay
Adoptive Mother12 weeksFull pay
Commissioning Mother (surrogacy)12 weeksFull pay
Miscarriage / Medical termination6 weeksFull pay

Under the Maternity Benefit Act, 1961 — applicable across India including Bihar — leave can start up to 8 weeks before the expected delivery date. The remaining weeks are taken after delivery.

To apply: Login to hrms.bihar.gov.in → Leave Management → Apply for Leave → Select Maternity Leave → Enter dates → Attach required documents (medical certificate of expected delivery date) → Submit to DDO.

Child Care Leave (CCL) Bihar Government — Affidavit Format

Child Care Leave or CCL is one of the most under-utilized yet most important leave types for women Bihar government employees. Many employees do not apply for CCL simply because they do not know how, or because they are unsure about the Bihar government child care leave affidavit format.

CCL Rules for Bihar Government Women Employees

✅ Child Care Leave — Key Facts
Total 730 days in entire service life (not per year). For children up to 18 years of age. For disabled children, age limit can be extended. Full pay for first 365 days, 80% pay thereafter. Cannot be taken for more than 3 spells in a calendar year. Requires DDO approval and CCL affidavit submission.

Bihar Government Child Care Leave Affidavit Format

The CCL affidavit (self-declaration) must be submitted with your HRMS Bihar leave application. There is no universally printed form — it is a written declaration. The format required by most Bihar departments is as follows:

⚠️ Important Note on CCL Affidavit
Submit this affidavit in writing to your DDO along with supporting documents — child's birth certificate, medical certificate if for illness. Some departments have their own official form — check with your HR section first. Once submitted physically, also submit the leave application through HRMS Bihar portal to create the digital record.

How to Cancel a Leave Application on HRMS Bihar

Plans change. You applied for a week's earned leave but the situation resolved sooner. Or your DDO needs you back early. Leave cancellation on the HRMS Bihar portal is simple — but it must be done before the leave end date.

  1. Login to hrms.bihar.gov.in → Leave Management → My Leave Applications.
  2. Find the approved leave entry you want to cancel. Click on it to expand details.
  3. Click Cancel Leave or Withdrawal Request.
  4. Enter the reason for cancellation and click Submit.
  5. The system notifies your DDO. Once DDO approves the cancellation, the leave days are restored to your balance automatically.
ℹ️ Leave and Salary — What Changes When You Take LWP or HPL
Your leave type directly impacts your monthly salary. Earned Leave and Casual Leave have zero salary impact. Half Pay Leave deducts 50% of salary for those days. LWP deducts 100% of salary. These deductions appear in your next payslip. Track them through the salary certificate section. If DA or increment changes during a leave period, check our DA arrear guide.

Frequently Asked Questions — HRMS Bihar Leave Application 2026

How to apply for leave online on HRMS Bihar portal?+
Login to hrms.bihar.gov.in → Leave Management → Check Leave Balance first → Apply for Leave → Select leave type → Enter dates and reason → Submit. Process takes under 3 minutes. DDO gets notified automatically. No separate HRMS leave management login is needed — same credentials as main portal.
What are the types of leave in Bihar government?+
Bihar government employees are entitled to: Earned Leave (30 days/year), Casual Leave (12 days/year), Half Pay Leave (20 days/year), Commuted Leave (from HPL balance), LWP / Extraordinary Leave (Leave Without Pay), Maternity Leave (26 weeks for first two children), Paternity Leave (15 days), Child Care Leave (730 days lifetime), Study Leave, and Special Casual Leave for specific purposes like election duty.
What is LWP in HRMS Bihar?+
LWP in HRMS Bihar is Leave Without Pay — also called Extraordinary Leave or EOL. It is applied when all your paid leave balances are exhausted, or when you voluntarily need unpaid leave. During LWP, your full salary is deducted including DA and allowances. The period may not count as qualifying service for pension. On HRMS Bihar portal it appears as LWP, EOL, or Leave Without Pay in the leave type dropdown.
What are maternity leave rules in Bihar government?+
Under the Maternity Benefit Act 1961 applicable in Bihar, women government employees get 26 weeks of fully paid maternity leave for their first two children. Third child onwards gets 12 weeks. Adoptive mothers get 12 weeks. Leave can start 8 weeks before expected delivery. Apply through HRMS Bihar — Login → Leave Management → Apply for Leave → Maternity Leave → attach delivery date medical certificate.
What is Bihar government child care leave affidavit format?+
The CCL affidavit is a self-declaration submitted to your DDO with the HRMS Bihar leave application. It must include: your name, designation, department, child's name and date of birth, leave dates, purpose of leave, and a declaration that total CCL availed is within the 730-day lifetime limit. See the full format on this page above. Check with your department HR section as some departments have their own version of this form.
My leave is rejected on HRMS Bihar — what to do?+
Login → Leave Management → My Leave Applications → open the rejected application. Your DDO's rejection reason is written there. Read it carefully. Common reasons: insufficient balance, incomplete supporting documents, overlapping with department requirements. Address the reason and reapply with complete documentation. If you believe the rejection is unjustified, escalate to your department head or file a complaint through HRMS helpline.
Can I apply for Casual Leave without reason on HRMS Bihar?+
Casual Leave is meant for personal and domestic reasons. While the HRMS Bihar form does have a reason field, Casual Leave does not require extensive documentation unlike Medical or Study Leave. A brief, honest reason like "personal work" or "family matter" is generally accepted. However it is still subject to your DDO's discretion and your department's operational requirements.
How many earned leave days can I carry forward in Bihar government?+
Bihar government employees can carry forward Earned Leave up to a maximum of 300 days in their entire service. 30 EL days are credited each year (15 on Jan 1 + 15 on Jul 1). Unused EL accumulates up to the 300-day ceiling. At retirement, accumulated EL up to 300 days is fully encashable — this is often a significant retirement benefit for long-serving employees.
