HRMS Bihar Leave Application Online 2026 – Complete Guide

Apply for earned leave, medical leave, and casual leave directly through the HRMS Bihar portal. No paperwork, no office visits — everything done online.

⏱ 4 min read📅 Updated February 2026✅ HRMS 2.0
  1. Home

⚡ Quick Answer
Login to hrms.bihar.gov.in → Leave Management → Apply Leave → Fill form → Submit. Your DDO gets notified automatically.

Online Leave System in HRMS Bihar

One of the biggest improvements in HRMS 2.0 Bihar is the fully digital leave management system. Bihar government employees no longer need to fill paper forms and physically submit them to their office. The entire leave application, approval, and tracking process is now available on hrms.bihar.gov.in.

According to Bihar government circulars, all departments have been directed to process leave applications through HRMS Bihar portal. This reduces paperwork, speeds up approvals, and creates a transparent digital record of your complete leave history. For background on how government leave systems work, see Wikipedia's overview of leave of absence policies.

✅ Types of Leave You Can Apply Through HRMS Bihar
Earned Leave (EL), Casual Leave (CL), Medical Leave (ML), Half Pay Leave (HPL), Maternity/Paternity Leave, Study Leave, Special Casual Leave — all can be applied through the HRMS Bihar portal.

How to Apply for Leave on HRMS Bihar Portal

Before applying, make sure you are logged into the HRMS Bihar portal. Then follow these steps:

  1. After login, navigate to the Leave Management section on your dashboard. It may appear as 'Leave' or 'Leave Application' in the menu.
  2. Click 'Apply for Leave'. A new form will open.
  3. Select the Leave Type from the dropdown — Earned Leave, Casual Leave, Medical Leave, etc.
  4. Enter the From Date and To Date of your leave. The system will automatically calculate the number of days.
  5. Fill in the Reason for Leave. For medical leave, you may need to attach a medical certificate.
  6. Click Submit. Your leave application is sent to your DDO/reporting officer for approval. You will receive confirmation on your registered mobile.
⚠️ Leave Balance Check
Before applying, always check your available leave balance. On HRMS Bihar, go to Leave Management → Leave Balance to see how many days of each leave type you have remaining. Applying for more leave than your balance may get rejected automatically.

How to Check Leave Application Status

  1. Login to hrms.bihar.gov.in and go to Leave Management.
  2. Click 'Leave Status' or 'My Leave Applications'.
  3. You will see all pending, approved, and rejected leave applications with dates and approver comments.

Leave Cancellation on HRMS Bihar

If you want to cancel an approved leave, go to Leave Management → My Leave Applications → find the approved leave → click Cancel. The system will notify your reporting officer. Once cancelled, the leave days are credited back to your leave balance. Also check our guide on viewing your salary slip to see how leave without pay affects your monthly pay.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I apply for leave in advance on HRMS Bihar?+
Yes. You can apply for future dated leave on HRMS Bihar portal. Enter the future start and end dates in the leave application form. The application goes to your DDO for approval immediately, so they can plan accordingly.
What happens if my leave is rejected on HRMS Bihar?+
If your leave application is rejected, you will receive a notification on your registered mobile number. Login to HRMS Bihar, go to Leave Status to see the rejection reason entered by your DDO. You can reapply after addressing the reason, or discuss with your reporting officer directly.
My leave is approved but salary shows leave deduction — why?+
This happens when leave is approved but the type was Half Pay Leave or Leave Without Pay, where salary deductions apply. Check your HRMS Bihar salary slip for the specific deduction code. Contact your DDO if there is a discrepancy.
