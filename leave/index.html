HRMS Bihar Leave Application Online 2026 – Apply, Track & Manage Leave

Apply for earned leave, medical leave, and casual leave through the HRMS Bihar portal. No paperwork, no office visits — complete digital leave management.

Digital Leave Management on HRMS Bihar

HRMS Bihar has made the leave application process completely paperless for Bihar government employees. All leave types — Earned Leave, Casual Leave, Medical Leave, Half Pay Leave, Maternity Leave — can be applied, tracked, and managed through the official HRMS Bihar portal at hrms.bihar.gov.in. Bihar government has directed all departments to process leave through HRMS only.

This digital system creates a tamper-proof record of your complete leave history, speeds up approval, and eliminates paperwork confusion. Government employees across India have been discussing the benefits of digital leave systems on forums like Reddit r/india.

✅ All Leave Types on HRMS Bihar
Earned Leave (EL), Casual Leave (CL), Medical Leave (ML), Half Pay Leave (HPL), Maternity Leave, Paternity Leave, Study Leave, Child Care Leave, Special Casual Leave — all managed through the HRMS Bihar portal at hrms.bihar.gov.in.

How to Apply for Leave on HRMS Bihar — Step by Step

  1. Login to hrms.bihar.gov.in. If you need login help, the HRMS Bihar portal guide covers the complete process.
  2. On your dashboard, look for Leave Management in the navigation menu. Click it.
  3. Click Apply for Leave. A leave application form opens.
  4. Select your Leave Type from the dropdown — Earned Leave, Casual Leave, Medical Leave, etc.
  5. Enter From Date and To Date. The system automatically calculates number of days and deducts from your balance.
  6. Enter the Reason for leave. For Medical Leave, attach your doctor's certificate if required by your department.
  7. Click Submit. Your application instantly goes to your DDO for approval. You get a confirmation on your registered mobile number.
⚠️ Check Leave Balance Before Applying
Go to Leave Management → Leave Balance before applying. This shows available days for each leave type. If you apply for more days than your balance, the application may auto-reject. For incorrect leave balance, contact your DDO or the HRMS Bihar helpline.

How to Check Leave Application Status

  1. Login to hrms.bihar.gov.in and go to Leave Management.
  2. Click My Leave Applications or Leave Status.
  3. All applications show with current status — Pending, Approved, or Rejected — with DDO comments visible.

Leave and Salary — What to Know

Your leave type directly affects your salary. Casual leave and earned leave have no salary impact. Half Pay Leave deducts 50% salary for the leave period. Leave Without Pay (LWP) deducts full salary for those days. These deductions reflect in your monthly payroll — you can track them through the salary certificate section. If DA or increment changes during a leave period, check our DA arrear guide for how it affects your pay.

Leave Cancellation on HRMS Bihar

To cancel an approved leave: Login → Leave Management → My Leave Applications → find the approved entry → click Cancel. The system notifies your DDO and the leave days are restored to your balance. For mobile access to manage leave on the go, see the HRMS Bihar mobile access guide.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I apply for leave in advance on HRMS Bihar?+
Yes. You can apply for future-dated leave on the HRMS Bihar portal. Enter the future start and end dates in the leave application form. The application goes to your DDO immediately for advance planning and approval.
My leave is rejected on HRMS Bihar — what to do?+
Login → Leave Status to see the rejection reason entered by your DDO. Address the reason and reapply, or discuss directly with your reporting officer. If you believe the rejection is unjustified, you can escalate through your department's service rules.
Can I apply for leave on HRMS Bihar from mobile phone?+
Yes. Open hrms.bihar.gov.in in Google Chrome on your Android or iPhone. The complete leave management section works on mobile. See the HRMS Bihar mobile guide for setup tips.
