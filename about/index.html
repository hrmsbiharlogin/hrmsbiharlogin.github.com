⚠️ Important Legal Notice – Please Read First
This website (hrmsbiharlogin.github.io) is a completely independent, privately operated informational website. It is NOT the official HRMS Bihar government portal. It is NOT affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by the Government of Bihar, the Bihar Finance Department, the National Informatics Centre (NIC), or any government body whatsoever. The official HRMS Bihar portal is at hrms.bihar.gov.in.

Who We Are

We are an independent team of digital content creators and researchers based in India. We run this website as a free informational resource to help Bihar state government employees understand and use the HRMS Bihar portal more easily.

We are private citizens — not government employees, not government contractors, and not affiliated with any government department. We do not represent the Government of Bihar in any capacity whatsoever.

What This Website Does

This website provides step-by-step written guides, explanations, and tutorials about how to use the official HRMS Bihar portal at hrms.bihar.gov.in. Think of us like a helpful friend who explains how to use a government website — we do not replace the government website, we simply help people understand it.

Specifically, we cover:

  • How to login to hrms.bihar.gov.in — step by step in English and Hindi
  • How to download salary slips from the HRMS portal
  • How to reset a forgotten password on HRMS Bihar
  • How to access service book and leave management features
  • HRMS Bihar information for teachers (Education Department)
  • HRMS 2.0 Bihar — what is new and how to use it
  • Common login problems and how to fix them

We do not provide any government services ourselves. All actual HR tasks must be performed on the official portal at hrms.bihar.gov.in.

Our Legal Standing Under Indian Law

Running an informational guide website about a government portal is fully legal under Indian law. Thousands of similar websites operate in India — SarkariResult.com, Jagran Josh, AajTak, NDTV — all regularly write about government portals, explain processes, and use government portal names. This is protected activity.

✅ Our Legal Protections — Why We Are Safe
  • ✓ IT Act Section 66D (Impersonation) does NOT apply — we do not impersonate the government or cheat any user
  • ✓ No phishing activity — we collect zero login credentials, zero personal data
  • ✓ State Emblem of India Act 2005 does NOT apply — we do not use the Ashoka Lion emblem or any government seal
  • ✓ Copyright protection — all our content is original, independently written
  • ✓ Clear independent identity — our domain, logo, and all text clearly show we are not a government site
  • ✓ User intent protected — we actively direct users to the real official government portal, not away from it
  • ✓ Public interest content — helping citizens use government services is clearly in the public interest
