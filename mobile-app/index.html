HRMS Bihar Mobile App 2026 – Access Portal on Android & iPhone

How to use HRMS Bihar on your smartphone in 2026 — official app status, the correct browser method, home screen shortcut, and all features available on mobile.

⏱ 4 min read📅 Updated February 2026✅ HRMS 2.0
  1. Home
  2. Mobile App
⚡ Quick Answer
No separate app needed! Open hrms.bihar.gov.in in Google Chrome on your Android or iPhone. HRMS 2.0 is fully mobile-responsive. Add to home screen for an app-like experience.

Is There an Official HRMS Bihar Mobile App?

This is one of the most searched questions by Bihar government employees. The direct answer: as of 2026, there is no official standalone HRMS Bihar mobile app published on Google Play Store or Apple App Store by the Bihar government or NIC Bihar.

However, the good news: with HRMS 2.0 Bihar, the portal website hrms.bihar.gov.in is fully mobile-responsive. It works exactly like a native app when opened in Google Chrome on Android or Safari on iPhone. All features work smoothly without any installation. First-time users should check the complete HRMS Bihar portal guide and login instructions for setup help.

⚠️ Beware of Fake HRMS Bihar Apps on Play Store
Several unofficial apps on Google Play Store use names like "HRMS Bihar", "Bihar Salary Slip", or "Bihar Govt Employee Portal". These are NOT official. Never enter your Employee ID or password on any unofficial app — it could steal your account credentials. Always use only hrms.bihar.gov.in in your browser. Fake government app warnings are regularly raised on Reddit r/india.

How to Access HRMS Bihar on Mobile — Step by Step

  1. Open Google Chrome on your Android phone (or Safari on iPhone). Chrome gives the best experience for hrms.bihar.gov.in.
  2. Type hrms.bihar.gov.in in the address bar and press Enter. The mobile-optimised version loads automatically.
  3. Enter your Employee ID and Password and complete the CAPTCHA to login. For login help, the complete HRMS Bihar portal guide covers each step.
  4. All features now work — leave applications, GPF statement, salary certificate — everything.
  5. To add as home screen shortcut on Android: tap three-dot menu in Chrome → Add to Home Screen → Name it HRMS Bihar → Add. It appears like an app icon.
  6. On iPhone: tap Share icon in Safari → scroll down → Add to Home Screen → Add. Full-screen app-like experience.
✅ Home Screen Shortcut = App-Like Experience (PWA)
Adding hrms.bihar.gov.in to your phone's home screen creates a Progressive Web App (PWA). It opens full-screen without the browser address bar, loads faster with cached data, and behaves exactly like a native app — all without any Play Store installation or storage used.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does HRMS Bihar portal work on iPhone Safari?+
Yes. Open hrms.bihar.gov.in in Safari on your iPhone. All features including login, leave application, salary slip, and GPF statement work. For downloading documents, tap Share → Print → save as PDF. You can also add it to your home screen: Share → Add to Home Screen → Add.
HRMS Bihar is very slow on my mobile — how to fix?+
Try: switch between WiFi and mobile data (one may be faster), clear Chrome cache (Settings → Privacy → Clear Browsing Data → Cached images), close background apps, try during off-peak hours (early morning). For persistent portal issues, contact the HRMS Bihar helpdesk at 0612-2217601.
