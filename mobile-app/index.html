HRMS Bihar Mobile App 2026 – Complete Guide to Access on Android & iPhone

Everything Bihar government employees need to know about accessing HRMS Bihar on mobile — dedicated app, browser access, features, and tips for best experience.

⏱ 4 min read📅 Updated February 2026✅ HRMS 2.0
  1. Home
  2. Mobile App
⚡ Quick Answer
No separate app needed! Open hrms.bihar.gov.in in Google Chrome on your Android or iPhone. HRMS 2.0 is fully mobile-responsive.

Is There an Official HRMS Bihar Mobile App?

This is one of the most frequently asked questions by Bihar government employees. As of 2026, the HRMS Bihar portal at hrms.bihar.gov.in does not have a dedicated standalone mobile app on Google Play Store or Apple App Store that is officially released and maintained by the Bihar government.

However, the good news is that with HRMS 2.0 Bihar, the portal website is now fully mobile-responsive. This means it works exactly like a native app when opened in Chrome on your Android or iPhone — all features work smoothly without needing to install anything.

⚠️ Beware of Fake HRMS Bihar Apps
Several unofficial third-party apps on Play Store claim to be "HRMS Bihar" or "Bihar Salary Slip" apps. These are NOT official and could be data-stealing apps. Never enter your Employee ID or password on any app other than the official website hrms.bihar.gov.in. Always check the developer name before installing any government-related app. See discussions about fake government apps on Reddit r/india.

How to Access HRMS Bihar on Your Mobile Phone

  1. Open Google Chrome on your Android phone (or Safari on iPhone). Chrome gives the best experience for hrms.bihar.gov.in.
  2. Type hrms.bihar.gov.in in the address bar and press Enter. The mobile-optimised version of the portal will load automatically.
  3. Enter your Employee ID and Password and complete the CAPTCHA to login. Need help logging in? Check our complete login guide.
  4. Once logged in, all features — salary slip download, leave application, service book, GPF — work on mobile.
  5. To save as a shortcut on your home screen: tap the three-dot menu in Chrome → 'Add to Home Screen' → Name it 'HRMS Bihar' → Add. It will appear like an app icon on your phone.
✅ Add HRMS Bihar to Home Screen — Works Like an App!
Adding hrms.bihar.gov.in to your phone's home screen via Chrome creates what is called a PWA (Progressive Web App). It opens in full screen without the browser bar, loads faster, and feels exactly like a native app — without any installation from Play Store.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does HRMS Bihar work on iPhone?+
Yes. HRMS Bihar (hrms.bihar.gov.in) works on iPhone using Safari or Chrome browser. Open the website on your iPhone browser, login normally, and all features work. To add to your home screen on iPhone: tap the Share icon in Safari → 'Add to Home Screen' → Add.
HRMS Bihar website is very slow on mobile — how to fix?+
If HRMS Bihar is slow on mobile, try: switch from WiFi to mobile data or vice versa, clear Chrome cache (Settings → Privacy → Clear Browsing Data), close other open apps to free RAM, or try during off-peak hours (early morning). For persistent issues, check our troubleshooting guide.
