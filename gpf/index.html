HRMS Bihar GPF Statement 2026 – How to Check GPF Balance Online

Check and download your General Provident Fund statement from hrms.bihar.gov.in. Know your balance, interest earned, and withdrawal history — all online.

⚡ Quick Answer
Login to hrms.bihar.gov.in → Finance menu → GPF Statement → Select financial year → View complete balance. Takes 2 minutes.

What is GPF for Bihar Government Employees?

General Provident Fund (GPF) is a mandatory retirement savings scheme for Bihar state government employees recruited before January 1, 2004. Every month, a fixed percentage of your basic pay is automatically deducted and deposited into your personal GPF account. The Bihar government guarantees interest on this amount — and the entire balance including interest is returned at retirement.

Employees who joined after January 1, 2004 are covered under NPS (National Pension System) instead. If you are unsure which scheme applies to you, check your monthly salary — your deduction column clearly shows either "GPF" or "NPS". For background on how provident funds work in India, Wikipedia's article on General Provident Fund is a reliable reference. To access your account, you first need to login — see the HRMS Bihar Login 2026 complete guide if you need help.

✅ Who Has GPF in Bihar Government?
Bihar state government employees recruited before 1 January 2004 — teachers, police, revenue officers, health workers, administrative staff, and all other departments — are covered under GPF. Post-2004 employees are under NPS. Check your NPS guide here if you joined after 2004.

How to Check GPF Balance on HRMS Bihar Portal

  1. Open Google Chrome and visit hrms.bihar.gov.in. Enter your Employee ID and Password and login.
  2. On your dashboard, find the Finance section or look for GPF / Provident Fund in the navigation menu. Click it.
  3. From the submenu, select GPF Statement or GPF Account Details.
  4. A filter screen appears — select the Financial Year for which you need the statement. For example: 2025-26.
  5. Click View. Your full GPF statement loads showing opening balance, monthly credits, interest credited, withdrawals, and closing balance. Press Ctrl+P → Save as PDF to download.
⚠️ GPF Statement Not Showing?
First confirm you are a pre-2004 employee. If yes, clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete) and retry. Some districts have pending data migration into HRMS. Contact your District Treasury or reach the HRMS Bihar helpdesk at 0612-2217601 if problem persists.

What Your GPF Statement Shows

FieldWhat It Means
Opening BalanceGPF balance at the start of the financial year
Monthly SubscriptionDeduction from salary credited to GPF each month
Interest CreditedAnnual interest at government-declared rate (7.1% for 2025-26)
Withdrawals / AdvancesAny partial withdrawals made during the year
Closing BalanceYour total GPF savings at end of year — this is your retirement corpus

GPF Withdrawal — When and How

  1. Medical Emergency — Serious illness of self or dependent family member. No repayment required after 10 years of service.
  2. Education — Higher education of children. Allowed after 15 years of service.
  3. Marriage — Marriage of self or children. Maximum 50% of GPF balance or 3 months basic pay, whichever is less.
  4. Housing — Construction, purchase, or major repair of residence.
  5. Final Withdrawal at Retirement — Complete balance paid out. No tax on GPF final withdrawal.

Withdrawal requests are submitted in writing to your DDO — not directly through the HRMS Bihar portal. Carry your GPF statement as supporting document. For salary-related queries alongside GPF, the DA arrear guide and increment guide are also helpful.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the GPF interest rate for Bihar employees in 2026?+
GPF interest rate for Bihar state government employees follows the Central Government rate — 7.1% per annum for 2025-26. Interest is calculated on minimum monthly balance and credited annually on March 31. The rate is revised quarterly by the Ministry of Finance, India.
Can I check GPF balance without visiting any office?+
Yes. With HRMS Bihar portal, you can check your GPF balance anytime from home using hrms.bihar.gov.in — no office visit needed. The balance is updated in real time by the Bihar Finance Department.
Is GPF amount shown on HRMS Bihar guaranteed?+
Yes. GPF is a government-guaranteed savings scheme. Unlike NPS which has market risk, GPF balance is 100% guaranteed by the Bihar government. The amount shown on your HRMS Bihar statement is your exact legally guaranteed savings.
