HRMS Bihar Salary Certificate Download 2026 – Complete Guide

Download your official salary certificate from hrms.bihar.gov.in for bank loans, visa applications, government schemes, and official income proof.

⚡ Quick Answer
Login to hrms.bihar.gov.in → Reports or Payroll menu → Salary Certificate → Select period → Download PDF.

What is HRMS Bihar Salary Certificate?

A salary certificate is an official document issued to a Bihar government employee that states their current designation, basic pay, gross salary, and net take-home salary. It is generated through the HRMS Bihar portal at hrms.bihar.gov.in and carries the digital authentication of the Bihar Finance Department, making it accepted across banks, embassies, and government offices.

Unlike a salary slip which shows monthly deductions, a salary certificate is a consolidated document that shows your annual income and is typically issued for loan applications or official purposes.

✅ Where is Salary Certificate Used?
Bank home loan and personal loan applications, visa applications for foreign travel, government scheme eligibility, admission to government housing schemes, children's school/college fee concessions, and any official income proof requirement.

How to Download Salary Certificate from HRMS Bihar

  1. Login to hrms.bihar.gov.in with your Employee ID and password. Forgot password? See our password reset guide.
  2. On your dashboard, look for the Reports section, Payroll menu, or Employee Self Service section. Click on it.
  3. Look for 'Salary Certificate' or 'Income Certificate' option and click on it.
  4. Select the financial year or period for which you need the certificate.
  5. Click Generate or View Certificate. Your salary certificate will appear. Press Ctrl+P → Save as PDF to download.
⚠️ Certificate Not Available Online?
If salary certificate is not directly available through the HRMS Bihar portal, approach your DDO or HR section. They can generate and issue a signed salary certificate from the HRMS Bihar system. Some departments have not yet fully activated this feature in HRMS 2.0.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is HRMS Bihar salary certificate accepted by banks for loans?+
Yes. Salary certificates generated from hrms.bihar.gov.in are accepted by all major nationalized and private banks for home loans, personal loans, and vehicle loans. The document carries Bihar government's digital authentication. Most banks also ask for 3-6 months of salary slips alongside the certificate.
How long does a salary certificate remain valid?+
Typically, banks and embassies accept salary certificates not older than 3 months. Always generate a fresh certificate from the HRMS Bihar portal when you need it for any official purpose. The portal allows you to generate it anytime.
