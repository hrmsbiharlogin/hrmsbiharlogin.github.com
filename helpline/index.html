HRMS Bihar Official Helpdesk — All Contacts

The HRMS Bihar helpdesk is managed by NIC Bihar (National Informatics Centre) on behalf of the Bihar Finance Department. It handles all technical issues related to hrms.bihar.gov.in. For basic login issues, first try the self-help steps in the Bihar govt staff HRMS portal usage instructions — most common problems are solvable without calling.

Exactly Who to Contact for Which Problem

  1. Portal not loading, login error, CAPTCHA issues, account locked — Call HRMS Bihar helpdesk 0612-2217601. Also try self-fix: clear cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete), use Chrome Incognito, check CAPS LOCK. See mobile access guide if issue is on phone.
  2. Salary wrong, DA or increment not updated, salary slip error — Contact your DDO (Drawing and Disbursing Officer) first. Payroll is processed at DDO level. For DA queries see DA arrear guide. For increment see annual increment guide.
  3. New employee registration, Employee ID not received — Contact your department HR section or DDO. HRMS registration is not self-service — it is done by your DDO.
  4. Mobile number update, PRAN not linked, GPF account issue — Visit your District NIC Centre with a written application from your DDO and employee ID card. For GPF queries: GPF guide. For NPS PRAN: NPS guide.
  5. Leave not approved, leave balance wrong — First contact your DDO. If DDO is unresponsive, see the leave application guide for escalation steps.
  6. Salary certificate not generating — Contact DDO or check the salary certificate guide first.
  7. Formal unresolved complaint — File at CPGRAMS — Government of India's official grievance portal. All complaints are tracked with accountability.
✅ Before Calling — Fix 80% of Issues Yourself
Most HRMS Bihar problems are self-fixable without calling: clear Chrome cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete), try Incognito mode (Ctrl+Shift+N), check CAPS LOCK, try mobile data instead of WiFi, try a different browser. For mobile-specific issues, see the HRMS Bihar mobile guide.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the HRMS Bihar helpline number toll-free?+
No. 0612-2217601 is a standard landline number charged at normal call rates. It is not toll-free. For non-urgent issues, email hrmshelp-bih@nic.in — it is free and gives you a written response trail for follow-up.
How long does HRMS Bihar helpdesk take to resolve issues?+
Simple issues — account unlock, login guidance — resolved on the same call. Backend technical issues: 2-5 working days. Data updates like mobile number change through District NIC: 7-14 working days. If unresolved beyond 15 days, file at CPGRAMS for accountability tracking.
What details to keep ready before calling HRMS Bihar helpdesk?+
Keep ready: Employee ID (HRMS ID), Department name, District, Designation, Registered mobile number, and a clear description of the exact error or problem. This helps the helpdesk resolve your issue on the first call without repeated follow-ups.
