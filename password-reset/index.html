HRMS Bihar Password Reset 2026 – Forgot Password Complete Guide

Reset your HRMS Bihar login password in just 4 steps using your registered mobile OTP. Works for all Bihar government employees.

⏱ 3 min read📅 Updated February 2026✅ HRMS 2.0
  1. Home
  2. Password Reset
⚡ Quick Answer
hrms.bihar.gov.in → Forgot Password → Enter Employee ID → Enter registered mobile → Get OTP → Set new password → Done! Takes 2 minutes.

HRMS Bihar Forgot Password — Step by Step

If you have forgotten your HRMS Bihar login password, do not worry. The portal has a simple OTP-based password reset system. Here is exactly what to do:

  1. Open hrms.bihar.gov.in in Chrome. On the login page, look for the 'Forgot Password' link — it is usually placed just below the Login button.
  2. A new screen will appear. Enter your Employee ID (HRMS ID) — the same ID you use to login normally.
  3. Enter your registered mobile number — this must be the exact number linked to your HRMS account when you registered. Click 'Send OTP'.
  4. A 6-digit OTP will arrive on your mobile. Enter it in the OTP field. Then type your new password, confirm it, and click Submit. Your new password is active immediately.
✅ Password Requirements
Your new HRMS Bihar password must be at least 8 characters long and should include uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and a special character (like @, #, $). Example: Bihar@2026

What If Mobile Number Is Not Registered?

This is the most common problem Bihar government employees face. If your registered mobile number has changed or was never linked, self-reset is not possible. Here is what to do:

  1. Write a formal application to your DDO (Drawing and Disbursing Officer) requesting mobile number update and password reset on HRMS Bihar.
  2. Your DDO will contact the District NIC Centre to update your mobile number in the system.
  3. Once updated, you will receive an OTP on your new number to complete the password reset process.
  4. If the process is taking too long, call the official HRMS Bihar helpdesk at 0612-2217601 directly and explain your situation.
⚠️ OTP Not Received?
Check your network signal first. Make sure you entered the correct mobile number. Wait 2-3 minutes — OTP sometimes takes time. Try again after clearing browser cache with Ctrl+Shift+Delete. If still not received after 3 attempts, contact helpdesk.

How to Change HRMS Bihar Password (When You Know Current Password)

If you know your current password but want to change it for security reasons, the process is different from password reset:

  1. Login to hrms.bihar.gov.in normally with your current Employee ID and password.
  2. After login, go to your Profile or Account Settings — usually accessible by clicking your name or profile icon at the top right.
  3. Find the 'Change Password' option. Enter your current password, then your new password, confirm it, and save.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many times can I reset HRMS Bihar password? +
There is no fixed limit on password resets on HRMS Bihar portal. However, too many failed login attempts may temporarily lock your account. If your account is locked, wait 30 minutes or contact helpdesk at 0612-2217601.
HRMS Bihar login says password expired — what to do? +
HRMS Bihar portal periodically asks users to update their password for security. If you see a password expired message, simply follow the on-screen instructions to set a new password. Your Employee ID remains the same.
I forgot both Employee ID and password — what to do? +
If you have forgotten your Employee ID as well, contact your department HR cell or DDO. Your Employee ID (HRMS ID) is also printed on your salary slip. Check any old salary slip you may have saved.
