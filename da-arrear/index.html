HRMS Bihar DA Arrear 2026 – Dearness Allowance Arrear Status & Calculation

Complete guide on DA arrear for Bihar government employees — how to check payment status, calculate arrear amount, understand tax rules, and what to do if DA arrear is not credited.

What is DA Arrear for Bihar Government Employees?

Dearness Allowance (DA) is an inflation-compensation payment made to Bihar government employees. The DA rate is revised twice yearly — effective January 1 and July 1 — based on the All India Consumer Price Index (AICPI). When Bihar announces a DA hike but implements it after the effective date, the salary difference for the intervening months is paid as DA arrear.

For example: if DA increases by 4% effective January 1, but is actually paid from March, then January and February arrear (2 months of higher DA) is credited as a lump sum in March's salary. This arrear appears as a separate line item on your HRMS Bihar payroll. To check your salary details on the portal, follow the HRMS Bihar employee dashboard complete portal guide. The Wikipedia article on Dearness Allowance explains how the AICPI formula works nationally.

✅ How to Check if DA Arrear is Credited
Login to hrms.bihar.gov.in → Payroll → Download salary for the relevant month. In the Earnings column, look for "DA Arrear" or "Dearness Allowance Arrear" as a separate line item. The amount shown is your total DA arrear for the arrear period.

DA Arrear Calculation — With Example

StepFormulaExample (Basic ₹40,000)
New DA per monthBasic × New DA% ÷ 100₹40,000 × 55% = ₹22,000
Old DA per monthBasic × Old DA% ÷ 100₹40,000 × 51% = ₹20,400
Monthly differenceNew − Old₹22,000 − ₹20,400 = ₹1,600
Total DA ArrearMonthly diff × Arrear months₹1,600 × 2 months = ₹3,200
⚠️ DA Arrear Not Credited — What to Do
DA arrear payment timelines vary by department. If colleagues received it but you have not, check with your DDO — there may be a payroll processing issue for your account specifically. Contact the HRMS Bihar helpdesk at 0612-2217601 if DDO cannot resolve it.

DA Arrear Tax Rules — Section 89(1) Relief

  1. DA arrear is fully taxable as salary income in the year it is received.
  2. To avoid paying excess tax on lump-sum arrear, claim Section 89(1) relief under Income Tax Act.
  3. File Form 10E on the Income Tax portal (incometax.gov.in) before you file your annual ITR.
  4. If you skip Form 10E, the IT department will issue a tax demand notice — this is a common mistake among government employees.

Your annual increment and DA hike often happen together, significantly boosting your gross salary. After any DA change, regenerate your HRMS Bihar salary certificate before applying for bank loans — lenders want your current income, not last year's figures. DA changes also affect NPS contributions slightly.

Frequently Asked Questions

When will Bihar DA arrear 2026 be paid?+
DA arrear payment dates depend on when Bihar government officially notifies the DA hike and when your DDO processes it in HRMS Bihar payroll. Bihar typically follows Central Government DA announcements within 1-3 months. Monitor official Bihar government notifications at the Bihar government official website for updates.
Does DA arrear affect GPF deduction?+
No. DA arrear is a one-time lump sum payment. Regular GPF and NPS deductions are based on basic pay only — not on DA or DA arrear. Receiving DA arrear does not change your monthly GPF deduction. See our GPF guide for more details on how GPF deductions are calculated.
