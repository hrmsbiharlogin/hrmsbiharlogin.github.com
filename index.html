What is HRMS Bihar? (hrms.bihar.gov.in Portal Overview)

HRMS stands for Human Resource Management System. Bihar government launched the HRMS Bihar portal as part of its digital governance initiative to bring all HR-related functions for state employees under one roof.

Before HRMS Bihar came into existence, government employees had to physically visit their departments or treasuries for the smallest HR tasks — getting a salary certificate, checking leave balance, or updating personal details. It was time-consuming, full of paperwork, and honestly quite frustrating. But now HRMS Bihar has changed all of that.

Today, the portal serves over 6 lakh government employees across Bihar — covering departments like Education, Health, Police, PWD, Revenue, Agriculture, Home, and many more. Whether you are a school teacher in Muzaffarpur or a revenue employee in Bhagalpur, or in Motihar or Ghorasahan, the HRMS Bihar portal is your central HR system.

HRMS Bihar Login – Step-by-Step Process

Alright, let us get to the practical part. Here is exactly how to login to the HRMS Bihar portal — whether you are on a desktop computer, a laptop, or your mobile phone.

HRMS Bihar Login on Desktop (hrms.bihar.gov.in)

The desktop browser is the most reliable way to access all features of the HRMS Bihar portal. Follow these steps:

HRMS Bihar Login Page — hrms.bihar.gov.in

  1. Open any browser (Google Chrome is recommended). In the address bar, type: hrms.bihar.gov.in and press Enter.
  2. The HRMS Bihar homepage will load. Look for the Login button — it is usually visible at the top right corner or in the centre of the page.
  3. Click on Login. A login form will appear asking for your Employee ID (also called HRMS ID or User ID).
  4. Enter your Employee ID carefully. This is an 8-digit alphanumeric code provided to you by your department.
  5. Enter your Password in the next field.
  6. A CAPTCHA code will appear on screen. Type it correctly in the CAPTCHA box.
  7. Click the Submit or Login button.
  8. If your credentials are correct, you will be taken directly to your HRMS Bihar employee dashboard.
💡 Pro Tip: If you see a blank screen or the page does not load fully, try pressing Ctrl + Shift + Delete to clear your browser cache and cookies. Then reload hrms.bihar.gov.in and try again. This fixes 90% of loading issues.

HRMS Bihar Login on Mobile

With HRMS 2.0 Bihar, the portal is now properly mobile-friendly. Most features work well on your smartphone. Here is how to login on mobile:

  1. Open Google Chrome on your Android phone (or Safari on iPhone).
  2. In the address bar, type hrms.bihar.gov.in and go.
  3. The mobile version of the portal will load automatically in HRMS 2.0.
  4. Tap the Login button. Enter your Employee ID, Password, and CAPTCHA.
  5. Tap Login. Your dashboard will open.

If certain features do not load properly on mobile, tap the three dots (menu) in Chrome and select 'Desktop Site'. This gives you the full portal experience.

HRMS Bihar Login Portal Registration (New Employee)

New to the HRMS Bihar portal? If you are a freshly appointed Bihar government employee and do not yet have login credentials, here is what you need to know.

⚠️ Important: Self-registration is not allowed on the HRMS Bihar portal. Registration is done through official HR channels only. Your department's HR cell or DDO (Drawing and Disbursing Officer) initiates and completes the registration process.

However, here is what happens and what you need to provide:

  • Active Employee ID — this is assigned to you at the time of joining.
  • Aadhaar-linked mobile number — must be the number registered with your Aadhaar.
  • Basic documents — appointment letter, service book, Aadhaar card.

Once your DDO completes the registration, you receive your User ID and a temporary password on your registered mobile number and email. Use these to login for the first time and then immediately change your password.

If your registration seems stuck or your DDO has not yet seeded your data into the system, visit your district NIC (National Informatics Centre) office or your department HR cell with a written application and a copy of your service book.

HRMS Bihar Login Salary Slip Download 2026

The salary slip feature is the most used part of the HRMS Bihar portal — and rightfully so. Every month, lakh of employees login specifically to download their HRMS Bihar login salary slip. Here is how to do it correctly.

How to Download Salary Slip Online – Step-by-Step

HRMS Bihar Salary Slip Download Section

  1. Login to hrms.bihar.gov.in using your Employee ID and Password.
  2. On your dashboard, look for the 'Payroll' section or 'Salary' menu. Click on it.
  3. Select 'Salary Slip' from the dropdown or submenu.
  4. A filter will appear — select the Financial Year and the Month for which you want the salary slip.
  5. Click on 'View Salary Slip'. The salary slip will load on your screen.
  6. Review the details — check your basic pay, DA, HRA, deductions, and net pay.
  7. Click 'Download' or 'Print' to save a copy. You can save it as PDF directly from your browser.

HRMS Bihar Login Salary Slip PDF Download

To get the HRMS Bihar login salary slip PDF — the format most useful for official submissions, bank loan applications, and income tax filing — follow this method:

  1. After the salary slip loads on screen (follow steps above), press Ctrl + P (Windows) or Cmd + P (Mac).
  2. Your browser's print dialog will open. Under 'Destination' or 'Printer', select 'Save as PDF'.
  3. Click 'Save'. Choose a location on your computer to save the file.
  4. Your HRMS Bihar salary slip PDF is now saved and ready to use.

On mobile, tap the Share icon or the three-dot menu after viewing the salary slip, and select 'Download' or 'Print to PDF'. The salary slip PDF can be used for ITR filing, loan applications, and official correspondence.

📌 Note: Some employees report that the HRMS Bihar portal allows password-protected PDF downloads. If this option appears, the password is typically your Employee Code. Check the download prompt on your portal dashboard.

HRMS Bihar Login Salary Slip Online – View Without Downloading

There are many people who not everyone wants to save a PDF every single time. If you simply need to check your salary details — say, to verify your net pay or check if a particular deduction was applied — you can view your HRMS Bihar salary slip online directly on screen.

Just follow steps 1 to 6 above (stop at 'View Salary Slip'). The slip displays fully in your browser. You can scroll through all details without downloading anything. This is also useful when you are on a mobile data connection and want to save data.

HRMS Bihar Education Department Teacher Login – Bihar Shikshak Portal

Teachers are one of the largest user groups on the HRMS Bihar portal. The Bihar Education Department employs lakhs of teachers — from primary school teachers to secondary and higher secondary faculty — and all of them access their HR services through the same HRMS Bihar login portal.

How Teachers Login to HRMS Bihar Portal

There is no separate HRMS Bihar teacher login portal or URL. Teachers use the exact same process as any other state government employee:

  1. Visit hrms.bihar.gov.in.
  2. Enter your Teacher Employee ID (HRMS ID assigned by Bihar Education Department).
  3. Enter your Password.
  4. Solve the CAPTCHA and click Login.

Your Teacher HRMS ID is different from your Niyojit Shikshak ID in some cases. If you are unsure which ID to use, check with your Block Education Officer (BEO) or your school's DDO. They have the correct HRMS Employee Code for your account.

HRMS Bihar Education Department – Features Specific to Teachers

Once logged in, Bihar Education Department teachers can access these features that are particularly useful for them:

  • Monthly salary slip download — especially important for niyojit and niyamit teachers who need salary proof for various purposes.
  • Service book access — check your appointment date, promotions, transfers, and grade history all in one place.
  • Leave management — apply for earned leave (EL), casual leave (CL), or medical leave online without physical forms.
  • GPF statement — check your General Provident Fund contributions and balance.
  • Annual increment tracking — verify your increments have been processed correctly each year.
  • Transfer history — full record of all your posting and transfer orders.

Teachers often find the service book feature particularly valuable — it serves as the official digital record for pension applications and departmental promotions.

HRMS Bihar Teacher Salary Slip Download – Niyojit, Niyamit & BPSC Shikshak

Whether you are a Niyojit Shikshak (contract teacher appointed by Panchayat/Nagar Nikaya), a Niyamit Shikshak (regular government teacher), or a newly appointed BPSC teacher — your salary slip is available on the same HRMS Bihar portal at hrms.bihar.gov.in. The process is identical for all teacher categories. Here is how to do it step by step.

  1. Open hrms.bihar.gov.in on your phone or computer. Use Google Chrome for best results.
  2. Enter your Teacher Employee ID (your HRMS ID assigned by Bihar Education Department) and your Password. Solve the CAPTCHA and click Login.
  3. On your dashboard, look for the Payroll section or Salary menu. Click on it.
  4. Click on Salary Slip. A filter will appear — select the Financial Year and the Month you need.
  5. Click View Salary Slip. Your complete salary slip will load on screen showing Basic Pay, DA, HRA, NPS/EPF deductions, and Net Pay.
  6. Press Ctrl+P on keyboard (or tap Share on mobile) → Select Save as PDF → Save to your phone or computer. Done.
⚠️ Salary slip not showing? This usually means your DDO has not yet processed your salary for that month in the HRMS system. Contact your school's DDO or your Block Education Officer (BEO) and give them your Employee ID. Ask them to confirm your salary has been processed in HRMS Bihar for that month.

How to Get Your HRMS ID as a Bihar Teacher – Step by Step

Many teachers, especially newly appointed BPSC teachers and Niyojit Shikshak, do not have their HRMS ID yet. This is because HRMS registration for teachers is not self-service — it is done through your school and the Bihar Education Department system. Here is the exact process:

  1. Get the HRMS registration form from your Block Resource Centre (BRC) or Block Education Officer (BEO) office. This is a physical form where you fill in your personal details, appointment details, Aadhaar number, and mobile number.
  2. Fill the form carefully — make sure your name exactly matches your Aadhaar card, your mobile number is your Aadhaar-linked number, and your appointment date is correct as per your joining letter.
  3. Submit the form to your BRC/BEO along with photocopies of your appointment letter, Aadhaar card, and service book (if available).
  4. BRC/BEO forwards your data to the district NIC (National Informatics Centre) centre for seeding into the HRMS Bihar system.
  5. You receive your Employee ID and temporary password on your Aadhaar-linked mobile number via SMS within a few working days.
  6. Login to hrms.bihar.gov.in using the credentials received on SMS. Change your password immediately after first login.
💡 Important: The registration form is sometimes called the BSEB HRMS Form or Education Department HRMS Form. If your BRC does not have it, ask your District Education Office (DEO) directly. You can also check with your school's Head Teacher or Principal — they usually have the forms.

If you are stuck and cannot resolve your HRMS issue on your own, here are the exact people and offices you need to contact as a Bihar teacher:

FAQ — HRMS Bihar Education Department Teacher Login

Q. What is the HRMS Bihar education department teacher login URL? +

The login URL for all Bihar Education Department teachers — whether Niyojit Shikshak, BPSC Niyamit Shikshak, or any other category — is hrms.bihar.gov.in. There is no separate teacher login page. Go to hrms.bihar.gov.in, enter your Teacher Employee ID (assigned by Bihar Education Department) and password, solve the CAPTCHA, and click Login.

Q. Can Niyojit Shikshak download salary slip from HRMS Bihar? +

Yes. Niyojit Shikshak (contract teachers) who have been registered in the HRMS Bihar system can login to hrms.bihar.gov.in and download their monthly salary slip from the Payroll section. If your salary slip is not visible, it means your DDO has not processed salary for that month. Contact your BRC or BEO to follow up.

Q. How do I get my HRMS Employee ID as a Bihar government teacher? +

Your HRMS Employee ID is assigned by the Bihar Education Department. If you have not received it, go to your Block Resource Centre (BRC) or Block Education Officer (BEO) office with your appointment letter and Aadhaar card. Fill the HRMS registration form. Once your data is seeded into the HRMS system, you receive your Employee ID and a temporary password on your Aadhaar-linked mobile number via SMS.

Q. I am a BPSC teacher. How do I login to HRMS Bihar? +

Newly appointed BPSC teachers (Niyamit Shikshak) login to hrms.bihar.gov.in using the Employee ID and password received via SMS after your joining formalities are completed. If you have not received credentials, contact your district DEO (District Education Officer) office and ask about your HRMS registration status. Your registration is initiated by the Education Department after your joining — it may take a few weeks.

Q. My HRMS Bihar teacher login password is not working. What do I do? +

On the login page at hrms.bihar.gov.in, click Forgot Password. Enter your Employee ID and your registered mobile number — the OTP will come on the mobile number linked to your HRMS account. If OTP does not come, your mobile number is not updated in the system. Visit your BEO office with a written application, your Employee ID, and Aadhaar to update your mobile number and reset password manually.

Q. What is the difference between Niyojit Shikshak ID and HRMS Employee ID? +

Your Niyojit Shikshak ID is the ID assigned to you under the Niyojan scheme by your Panchayat or Nagar Nikaya. Your HRMS Employee ID is a separate 8-digit code assigned specifically for the HRMS Bihar portal by the Bihar Education Department. These are two different numbers. To login to hrms.bihar.gov.in, you need the HRMS Employee ID, not the Niyojit Shikshak ID. Contact your BRC if you are unsure which is which.

HRMS Bihar Service Book Online – How to Check

The HRMS Bihar service book online is essentially your entire government employment history captured in a digital format. Think of it as your official career file — every posting, promotion, salary revision, leave record, increment, and transfer is recorded here.

This is extremely important for pension processing, departmental promotions, and any official inquiry. Here is how to access your HRMS Bihar service book:

  1. Login to hrms.bihar.gov.in with your Employee ID and password.
  2. On the main dashboard, find the 'Service Book' option. It may be under a 'Services' or 'Employee Records' menu.
  3. Click on 'Service Book'. The system will load your complete digitised service record.
  4. You can view entries section by section — personal details, appointment history, promotions, transfers, disciplinary records (if any), and training records.
  5. Use the Print or Download option to get a PDF copy of your service book for official use.
📋 Important: The HRMS Bihar service book is now legally accepted as a valid official document for most departmental purposes including pension applications. Always keep a downloaded copy updated.

If you find any error in your service book — say, a missing increment or an incorrect promotion date — do not try to correct it yourself. Raise a grievance through the portal's 'Complaint' module or contact your DDO with documentary evidence. Service book corrections go through a formal approval chain.

HRMS Bihar Login Password Reset / Forgot Password

Forgetting your password is one of the most common problems on the HRMS Bihar portal — many people search about it, especially for employees who login only once a month to download their salary slip and end up forgetting the password in between. No need to panic. Here is exactly how to reset it.

HRMS Bihar Login Portal Forgot Password – Step-by-Step

HRMS Bihar Forgot Password Page

  1. Go to hrms.bihar.gov.in.
  2. On the login page, click on 'Forgot Password' — this link is usually just below the login button.
  3. A new screen appears. Enter your registered Employee ID (HRMS ID).
  4. Enter your registered mobile number — this must be the same number linked to your HRMS account when you registered.
  5. Click 'Send OTP'. You will receive a 6-digit OTP on your registered mobile number.
  6. Enter the OTP in the field provided.
  7. Set a new password. Use a combination of letters, numbers, and a special character (minimum 8 characters recommended).
  8. Confirm the new password and click 'Submit' or 'Change Password'.
  9. Your new password is active immediately. Login with your Employee ID and the new password.
⚠️ What if you no longer have access to your registered mobile number? If your mobile number has changed and is no longer registered on HRMS Bihar, self-reset is not possible. You will need to contact your DDO (Drawing and Disbursing Officer) with a written application requesting manual password reset. The DDO then coordinates with the district NIC centre to update your mobile number and reset the account.

How to Change HRMS Bihar Password (When You Are Already Logged In)

If you are already logged in and want to change your HRMS Bihar password proactively — for example, because you received a temporary password or want to update a weak password — here is how:

  1. Login to hrms.bihar.gov.in as usual.
  2. Once on your dashboard, click on your Profile name or the Account Settings icon (usually at the top right corner).
  3. Look for 'Change Password' option.
  4. Enter your Current Password.
  5. Enter your New Password twice (once to set, once to confirm).
  6. Click 'Save' or 'Update Password'.

Your password will be updated immediately. Use the new password for all future logins.

HRMS 2.0 Bihar – What Is New and How to Login

HRMS 2.0 Bihar is the current, active version of the portal. If you have been using HRMS Bihar for a few years, you may have noticed the interface has changed considerably. This is HRMS 2.0 — a comprehensive upgrade that the Bihar government pushed out to address the shortcomings of the first version.

Here is what is genuinely different about HRMS 2.0 Bihar:

  • Performance: The portal is faster and more stable. Earlier, the system would slow down or time out during peak usage — particularly on salary days. HRMS 2.0 handles higher traffic much better.
  • Mobile Design: HRMS 2.0 is built to work on smartphones. This was a major pain point in the earlier version. Now employees in remote districts can access their salary slips and service books from their phones without needing a PC.
  • Salary Slip Reliability: The salary slip PDF generation was unreliable in older versions. HRMS 2.0 fixed this — salary slips generate consistently and quickly.
  • AI-Assisted Leave Routing: Leave applications are now automatically routed to the appropriate officer based on department hierarchy, reducing unnecessary delays.
  • Biometric 2FA: For sensitive operations like salary revision requests, HRMS 2.0 now requires biometric verification through the HRMS Bihar Android App — an important security addition.
  • New Module Additions: HRMS 2.0 includes modules for car loans, laptop loans, and house-building advances that the original portal lacked.

How to Login to HRMS 2.0 Bihar

Nothing changes for the login process. The URL remains hrms.bihar.gov.in. Your Employee ID and Password remain the same. The portal automatically serves the HRMS 2.0 interface when you visit the site. No separate registration or setup is needed.

🔮 Future Roadmap: The Bihar government has announced plans for further upgrades including an AI-powered Hindi/Bhojpuri voice chatbot for query resolution, blockchain-secured service records, pension integration so retired employees can access their full history with a single login, and AI-driven skill mapping for promotion recommendations. These features are expected to roll out through 2026.

Frequently Asked Questions – HRMS Bihar Login

Here are the top questions Bihar government employees ask about the HRMS Bihar portal. We have answered each one clearly and completely.

Q1. What is the official HRMS Bihar login URL? +

The official URL is hrms.bihar.gov.in. Always use this URL directly. Do not use third-party links or unofficial websites. Bookmark the official link on your device for safe, quick access.

Q2. How do I download my HRMS Bihar salary slip? +

Login to hrms.bihar.gov.in → Go to Payroll / Salary menu → Select Salary Slip → Choose month and year → Click View Salary Slip → Download as PDF using Ctrl+P → Save as PDF. The process takes less than a minute once you are logged in.

Q3. I forgot my HRMS Bihar login password. What should I do? +

Click 'Forgot Password' on the login page at hrms.bihar.gov.in. Enter your Employee ID and your registered mobile number. An OTP will be sent to your mobile. Enter the OTP, set a new password, and confirm. If your mobile number is no longer registered, contact your DDO for manual reset.

Q4. Is there a separate HRMS Bihar teacher login portal? +

No. Teachers log in through the same portal at hrms.bihar.gov.in using their HRMS Employee ID and password. The Employee ID for teachers is assigned by the Bihar Education Department. If you do not have it, check with your Block Education Officer (BEO).

Q5. How do I check my HRMS Bihar service book online? +

After logging in to hrms.bihar.gov.in, navigate to the 'Service Book' section from your dashboard. Your complete digitised service history — appointments, promotions, transfers, increments — will be visible. You can download a PDF copy for official use.

Q6. What is HRMS 2.0 Bihar and do I need to register separately? +

HRMS 2.0 Bihar is the upgraded version of the HRMS portal with faster performance, mobile-friendly design, improved salary slip generation, and better security. You do not need to register separately. Just visit hrms.bihar.gov.in and you automatically get the HRMS 2.0 experience.

Q7. My HRMS Bihar login is not working. What are the steps to fix it? +

First, clear your browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete) and reload the page. Second, check that your CAPS LOCK is off when entering the password. Third, try using Google Chrome in Incognito mode. Fourth, if you have forgotten the password, use Forgot Password. Fifth, if the portal itself is down, wait an hour and try again. If none of these work, call the helpdesk at 0612-2217601.

Q8. How do I register on HRMS Bihar portal for the first time? +

Registration on HRMS Bihar is not self-service. It is done by your department's HR cell or DDO (Drawing and Disbursing Officer). Once registered, you receive your User ID and temporary password on your registered mobile number. If you have not received login credentials yet, contact your DDO or department HR office.

Q9. Can I access HRMS Bihar login on my mobile phone? +

Yes. HRMS 2.0 Bihar is mobile-responsive. Open hrms.bihar.gov.in in Google Chrome on your Android or iPhone, and most features — salary slip download, leave application, service book — work well. For the best experience on mobile, make sure you are on a stable internet connection.

Q10. What is the HRMS Bihar helpdesk number and support email? +

HRMS Bihar helpdesk number: 0612-2217601. Support email: [email protected]. Support hours: Monday to Saturday, 8 AM to 10 PM. For district-level hardware or software issues, visit your nearest District NIC Centre. For policy or legal disputes, contact the HR Directorate in Patna.

Conclusion

The HRMS Bihar portal has genuinely transformed how over 6 lakh state government employees interact with their HR systems. What used to take trips to the treasury, paperwork with DDOs, and weeks of waiting can now be done in minutes from your phone or computer at hrms.bihar.gov.in.

Whether you need to download a salary slip for a bank loan, check your service book for a pension application, apply for leave, or just verify your deductions for this month — the HRMS Bihar login portal handles it all. And with HRMS 2.0 Bihar now active and further upgrades planned, the system is only going to get better.
