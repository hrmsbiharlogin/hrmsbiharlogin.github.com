⚠️ GPF or NPS — Which Are You?
GPF applies only to Bihar government employees recruited before 1 January 2004. Post-2004 employees are under NPS. Check your salary slip — "GPF Deduction" means GPF, "NPS Deduction" means NPS. You cannot login to e-gpf.bihar.gov.in if you are an NPS employee. Check our NPS guide here if you joined after 2004.

Method 1: GPF Bihar Login via e-gpf.bihar.gov.in

This is the main, dedicated GPF portal for Bihar. Most employees who search "egpf bihar login" or "e-gpf.bihar.gov.in login" are looking for exactly this. The process is straightforward once you know your GPF account number.

e-GPF Bihar Portal Login Page at e-gpf.bihar.gov.in

  1. Open Google Chrome and go to e-gpf.bihar.gov.in — the official GPF Bihar portal by the Directorate of GPF, Finance Department, Government of Bihar.
  2. Enter your GPF Account Number as User ID. The format is SERIES/NUMBER — for example EDN/12345 for Education or MED/567 for Medical. Find this on your salary slip under the "GPF Deduction" column.
  3. Enter your Password. First-time login default password is abc (all lowercase). If already changed before, use your current password. Change the default password immediately after first login.
  4. After login, click Employees Corner on the homepage. Then click the GPF tab. Your GPF account details will load.
  5. Select the Financial Year — for example 2025-26. Click Generate GPF Slip. Your complete GPF statement appears on screen.
  6. Press Ctrl+P on keyboard. Change the print destination to Save as PDF. Click Save. Your GPF statement download is complete.

✅ Cannot find your GPF number on salary slip?
Contact your DDO (Drawing and Disbursing Officer) in your department — they have a register with every employee's GPF account number. Once your mobile number is registered in the GPF system, you will also receive SMS notifications with your account details.

If you cannot reset your e-GPF Bihar login password online because your mobile is not registered — download the Password Change Request Form from e-gpf.bihar.gov.in, get it signed by your DDO, and submit it to the GPF Directorate, Panth Bhawan, Bailey Road, Patna. Your nearest district treasury office also accepts it.

Method 2: HRMS GPF Statement via hrms.bihar.gov.in

If you already use the HRMS Bihar portal for your salary slip, leave application, or increment details — you do not need a separate login for GPF. The HRMS portal has a Finance menu that shows your GPF statement directly.

  1. Go to hrms.bihar.gov.in and login with your Employee ID and password.
  2. On the dashboard, find the Finance menu in the navigation. Click it and select GPF Statement or GPF Account Details from the submenu.
  3. A filter screen appears. Select the financial year — for example 2025-26. Click View.
  4. Your HRMS GPF statement loads with opening balance, monthly credits, interest, withdrawals, and closing balance. Press Ctrl+P → Save as PDF to download.
ℹ️ HRMS GPF Statement Not Showing?
First confirm you are a pre-2004 employee. If yes, clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete) and retry. Some districts have pending data migration into HRMS. Contact your District Treasury or reach the HRMS Bihar helpdesk at 0612-2217601 if the problem continues.

Method 3: e-GPF Statement via CFMS 2.0 — Karmchaari Seva Portal

The CFMS 2.0 (Comprehensive Financial Management System 2.0) at e-nidhi-v2.bihar.gov.in is Bihar's newest financial portal, launched in 2025. The Karmchaari Seva Portal is its employee self-service section — Karmchaari means employee in Hindi.

Think of it this way — e-gpf.bihar.gov.in handles GPF only. CFMS 2.0 handles GPF, NPS, salary, deductions, loans, pension, tax — everything in one login. It takes more steps to set up initially, but once done it is the most complete portal for a Bihar government employee's financial records.

CFMS 2.0 / Karmchaari Seva Portal — e-nidhi-v2.bihar.gov.in

  1. Open e-nidhi-v2.bihar.gov.in — this is the official CFMS 2.0 Karmchaari Seva Portal of Government of Bihar.
  2. Enter your User ID and Password. Then provide your PAN number, GPF/PRAN number, and Aadhaar number. Generate OTP via your registered mobile number to complete login.
  3. After login, find Payee Self Service or e-GPF Self Service on the dashboard — the exact label depends on your department and role.
  4. Click the Pay Bill link, then select Annual GPF Statement. Enter your GPF or PRAN number, select the financial year, and set Report Type to PDF.
  5. Click Generate. Your e-GPF statement download PDF saves automatically. This is your official annual GPF statement from the CFMS 2.0 system.

✅ GPF vs NPS — Key Difference on Returns
GPF gives a guaranteed 7.1% interest with zero market risk — the Bihar government guarantees every rupee. NPS returns depend on market-linked investments and can vary. This is why pre-2004 employees prefer GPF. GPF final withdrawal at retirement is also 100% tax-free under Section 10(11) of the Income Tax Act. For NPS details see our complete NPS guide.

GPF Withdrawal Rules for Bihar Government Employees

You can make partial GPF withdrawals — called GPF advances — during service for specific approved purposes. These are not taken lightly and each has conditions. Here is a clear summary.

🏥

Medical Emergency

Serious illness of self or dependent family. No repayment required after 10 years of service.

🎓

Higher Education

For children's higher education. Allowed after 15 years of service.

💍

Marriage

Marriage of self or children. Maximum 50% of GPF balance or 3 months basic pay — whichever is less.

🏠

Housing

Construction, purchase, or major repair of residence. Subject to available balance.

🛒

Consumer Goods

Purchase of consumer durables after 10 years of service. Limited amount allowed.

🎯

Final Withdrawal at Retirement

Complete balance paid out. 100% tax-free. No conditions or repayment.

📝 How to Apply for GPF Withdrawal in Bihar
GPF withdrawal applications are submitted in writing to your DDO (Drawing and Disbursing Officer) — not through e-gpf.bihar.gov.in or HRMS Bihar portal. Physical form is required. Always carry a printed copy of your downloaded GPF statement as supporting document with the application. For salary-related queries, also check our DA arrear guide.

Frequently Asked Questions — GPF Bihar Login & Statement 2026

What is the official website for GPF Bihar login?+
The official portal for GPF Bihar login is e-gpf.bihar.gov.in, maintained by the Directorate of GPF, Finance Department, Government of Bihar. GPF details can also be accessed from HRMS Bihar at hrms.bihar.gov.in and through CFMS 2.0 Karmchaari Seva Portal at e-nidhi-v2.bihar.gov.in. All three are official government portals.
What is the default password for e-GPF Bihar login?+
The default password for first-time login on e-gpf.bihar.gov.in is "abc" (all lowercase letters, no capitals). Your GPF account number — for example EDN/12345 — is the User ID. Change this default password immediately after your first login. If "abc" is not working, the password was likely already changed — contact your DDO or call 0612-2217601 for a reset.
How to check GPF balance enquiry Bihar online by mobile number?+
Visit e-gpf.bihar.gov.in on your mobile browser. Login with your GPF account number and password. Go to Employees Corner → GPF tab → select year → Generate GPF Slip. For password reset via OTP, your mobile number must be registered in the GPF system. If not registered, visit your DDO or district GPF office — this requires a physical visit and cannot be done online.
What is GPF interest rate for Bihar employees in 2025-26?+
GPF interest rate for Bihar state government employees is 7.1% per annum for 2025-26 — same as the Central Government rate declared by the Ministry of Finance, India. Interest is calculated on the minimum monthly balance and credited on 31st March each year. Unlike NPS, GPF balance has zero market risk and is 100% government-guaranteed.
What is CFMS 2.0 and how is it different from e-GPF Bihar?+
CFMS 2.0 (Comprehensive Financial Management System 2.0) at e-nidhi-v2.bihar.gov.in is Bihar's all-in-one financial portal with an e-GPF Self Service module. It also handles salary slip, NPS, tax, loans, and pension in one login. The dedicated e-gpf.bihar.gov.in is a GPF-only portal. Both are official and valid. If you only need GPF, use e-gpf.bihar.gov.in. For everything in one place, use CFMS 2.0.
What is the Karmchaari Seva Portal Bihar?+
Karmchaari Seva Portal — Karmchaari means employee in Hindi — is the self-service section of the CFMS 2.0 / e-Nidhi Bihar portal at e-nidhi-v2.bihar.gov.in. It allows Bihar state government employees to view and download their salary slip, GPF or NPS statement, service records, PAN details, and other financial records digitally without visiting any government office.
GPF statement not showing on HRMS Bihar — what to do?+
First confirm you are a pre-January 2004 recruit because GPF only applies to pre-2004 Bihar government employees. If confirmed, try: (1) Clear browser cache with Ctrl+Shift+Delete. (2) Use Chrome browser. (3) Try during off-peak hours — before 9am or after 7pm. (4) Some districts have pending data migration — contact your District Treasury. (5) If still unresolved, call 0612-2217601 or email hrmshelp-bih@nic.in.
Is GPF final withdrawal taxable for Bihar government employees?+
No. GPF final withdrawal at retirement is 100% tax-free under Section 10(11) of the Income Tax Act for government employees. The entire accumulated corpus — principal plus all interest earned — is fully exempt from income tax. This is a major advantage of GPF over NPS, where 40% of the corpus must be invested in an annuity at retirement.
Can I check Bihar GPF balance online without visiting any office?+
Yes, absolutely. All three official portals — e-gpf.bihar.gov.in, hrms.bihar.gov.in, and e-nidhi-v2.bihar.gov.in — provide 24×7 online access to your GPF balance and annual statement from home or mobile. You only need to visit the GPF Office Patna or your District Treasury for: password reset if mobile is not registered, GPF withdrawal application, account discrepancy correction, or physical duplicate statement.
Where is the GPF Office Patna Bihar — address and phone?+
GPF Office Patna address: GPF Directorate, 5th Floor, Panth Bhawan, Hartali More, Bailey Road, Patna – 800001, Bihar. HRMS Helpline: 0612-2217601. Email: hrmshelp-bih@nic.in. For district-level issues, contact your nearest District Treasury Office instead of travelling to Patna.
