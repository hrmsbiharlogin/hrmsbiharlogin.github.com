Privacy Policy

How this website handles your information — and why we collect almost none of it.

  1. Home
  2. Privacy Policy
📅 Last Updated: June 2026  |  🔒 Privacy Document  |  Applies to: hrmsbiharlogin.github.io
🔒 Our Core Privacy Commitment

This website does not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information from visitors. We have no login system, no sign-up form, no comment system, and no mechanism whatsoever to collect your personal data.

✓ No Employee IDs collected
✓ No passwords collected
✓ No names or emails collected
✓ No Aadhaar or phone numbers
✓ No account registration
✓ No data sold or shared

1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to the website hrmsbiharlogin.github.io. We are an independent, privately operated informational website. We are not the Government of Bihar, the National Informatics Centre, or any government body. For our full identity disclosure, see our About Us and Disclaimer pages.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be explicitly clear about what we do not collect:

  • Employee IDs, HRMS IDs, or any government employee identifiers
  • Passwords, PINs, OTPs, or security credentials of any kind
  • Aadhaar card numbers or biometric data
  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Bank account details or financial information
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Photographs, videos, or any media files
  • IP addresses stored in identifiable form
  • Any data that could personally identify you

There are no forms, no input fields, no comment boxes, and no account systems on this website that could capture your personal information.

3. Automatically Collected Technical Data

Like all websites on the internet, some minimal technical data may be processed automatically through third-party services we use:

  • GitHub Pages Hosting: This website is hosted on GitHub Pages (by Microsoft/GitHub). GitHub may collect standard server logs including IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited as part of their infrastructure. We do not access or control this data. See GitHub's Privacy Statement.
  • Google Fonts: We use Google Fonts for typography. When the page loads, your browser connects to Google's servers to download fonts. Google may collect minimal data as part of this. See Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any tracking scripts on this website.

4. Cookies

This website does not set any first-party cookies. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or session cookies. Google Fonts may set a minimal cookie as part of their service — this is outside our control. Your browser settings allow you to block all cookies if you prefer.

5. Google AdSense (If Applicable)

This website may display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google AdSense uses cookies and similar technologies to show relevant advertisements based on your interests. Google may collect information about your visits to this and other websites. This is governed by Google's own privacy policies.

You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or by using the NAI opt-out tool.

6. External Links

This website contains links to external websites including the official HRMS Bihar portal at hrms.bihar.gov.in, the Government of Bihar website, NIC India, and CPGRAMS. These websites have their own privacy policies which we do not control. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any external websites you visit.

7. Children's Privacy

This website is intended for adults — specifically Bihar state government employees. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 18 years of age. If you believe a child has provided any information through this website, please contact us immediately.

8. Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data, there is virtually no personal data to secure. The website is served over HTTPS (secure connection) via GitHub Pages, ensuring that all communication between your browser and our website is encrypted in transit.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, most data rights (access, deletion, correction) are not applicable. However, if you believe we have inadvertently collected any of your data, you have the right to request its deletion by contacting us. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We recommend checking this page periodically. Continued use of this website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

⚠️ Important Reminder
The ONLY official HRMS Bihar portal where you should enter your Employee ID and password is hrms.bihar.gov.in. Never enter your HRMS login credentials on any other website including this one. We do not have any login form and will never ask for your credentials.
